### Knight (Player)

```
Square image, 1:1 aspect ratio. The image has two zones stacked vertically: the TOP two-thirds is a solid flat magenta (#FF00FF) background — pure flat fill, no variation, no lines or borders of any kind between cells. The BOTTOM one-third is a solid black bar — completely empty, no characters, no colour. These two zones together make up the full image. Within the magenta area only, draw exactly 4 poses of the same knight arranged in four equal quadrants — top-left, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-right. Exactly 2 columns and 2 rows. Do not generate more than 2 columns. Do not repeat any pose. NOT a horizontal strip. NOT a 4×1 strip. Top-left cell: Idle. Top-right cell: Attack. Bottom-left cell: Hit. Bottom-right cell: Dead. Exactly one pose per quadrant, 4 poses total, nothing repeated. The 2×2 grid must be contained entirely within the coloured top section — the black bar at the bottom must remain completely empty. Style: flat 2D cartoon Pokémon-style game sprite. VERY THICK, VERY BOLD black outlines on every character edge — heavy enough for clean chroma-key removal. All colour effects must be fully enclosed within the black outlines — no glow, bloom, or light bleed outside the outlines. Solid flat colours only — no shading, no gradients, no lighting, no drop shadows, no textures, no 3D effects, no impact effects, no sparks, no motion blur, no speed lines. Character: cute, heroic, and friendly — this is the PLAYER character, a brave little champion the child is rooting for. He radiates cheerful determination, not menace. Rounded shapes, small neat confident eyes visible through the visor, warm friendly face. Polished silver plate armour with a warm sky-blue tint, bright gold trim and rivets on every plate edge, a scarlet-red plume on the rounded helmet, a round wooden shield with a gold boss, a straight sword with a silver blade and gold crossguard, bright blue eyes visible through the visor. Silver, gold, and red — colours that read immediately as the hero. Same character size in all 4 cells. Feet at the same vertical position in every cell. Poses — all facing RIGHT: Top-left: Idle — relaxed combat stance, sword held down at his side, shield raised slightly, feet planted, weight on back foot, calm and ready. Top-right: Attack — sword arm fully extended to the right, body weight driven forward on the front foot, leaning hard into the strike. No sparks, no impact flash, no effects at the sword tip — just the blade. Bottom-left: Hit — recoiling backwards, body leaning back, shield raised high defensively, free arm out for balance, slightly hunched and off-balance. Bottom-right: Dead — collapsed on the ground, slumped sideways, sword dropped, shield fallen, eyes closed. Fully defeated. No text, no labels, no watermarks, no floor, no ground plane, no scenery, no shadows of any kind.
```
