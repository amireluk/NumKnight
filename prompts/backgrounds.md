### 6. Start Screen — Castle & Fields (`start.webp`)

```
2D stylized game background, landscape 4:3 aspect ratio (wider than tall), painterly gouache style. Key visuals centered in the middle 50% of the image width — sides may be cropped on phones. Ground / horizon line HIGH in the frame at roughly 58% from the top. Full, rich saturation is allowed — this is not a battle arena so the grey-wash restriction is lifted, but the palette must still feel like a hand-painted fantasy illustration, not photorealistic or 3D-rendered. No characters, no text, no UI, no watermark, no border, no HDR glow. The bottom 8% of the image must be solid black — no scene content in that strip.

Daytime kingdom vista. Bright blue sky with warm haze near the horizon. A golden sun in the upper-right area, rendered as a soft diffuse glow with a subtle radiant halo — not a sharp disc. Two clusters of white-grey clouds: one larger cluster on the left at mid-sky height, one smaller cluster to the right of center. The full width of the lower half is covered by gently rolling green grass fields — three overlapping hill layers in progressively darker greens, with the furthest layer meeting the horizon at roughly 58% from the top.

CASTLE: A stone medieval castle sits DEAD CENTER in the mid-ground. It has a broad rectangular main keep with crenellated battlements across the top. Two slightly shorter square towers flank the keep, each capped with a conical pointed roof in dark blue-grey slate. A wide arched stone gateway is centered in the keep. Two flagpoles rise from the tops of the flanking towers, each flying a small triangular flag in deep red. The castle is rendered in warm sandstone — honey-beige and grey stone, with subtle shadow on the lower walls. The arched windows on the towers have a faint warm amber glow, as if lit from within. The castle sits on a low grassy mound, grounded into the landscape.

TREES: Pine tree clusters on the left side (roughly 0–25% of width) and right side (roughly 75–100% of width). Each cluster has 3–4 dark pine silhouettes with triangular canopies — the inner trees of each cluster extend to about 20% from the edge, framing the castle without obscuring it. Tree trunks are short and dark brown.

FOREGROUND: Flat lush grass in the immediate foreground fills the bottom 35%. A dirt path or stone road leads from the bottom center toward the castle gate — it narrows with perspective into the center mid-ground.

Palette: warm greens, sky blues, golden sunlight, warm sandstone castle, deep blue-grey roofs. Slightly desaturate the far hills relative to the foreground to create aerial perspective depth.
```
